GIGGLE

About

Giggle is a software company dedicated to making apps for kids. We have a diverse team full talented people, from all over the world. Which of whom, all have a passion for helping children. We are located in Long Island, New York. The difference between Giggle and other software companys, is the aim to help children learn and let them be creative.
